Yoo Jae Suk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


Yoo Jae-suk (Korean : 유재석 born on 14 August 1972) is a South Korean comedian, host and television personality currently signed to FNC Entertainment. He has hosted several variety television shows in 
South Korea, including Infinite Challange,Running Man,Happy Together and Hangout with Yoo. Known for his quick wit and appeal across a wide range of demographics, Yoo has established himself as one 
of South Korea's top comedians and TV personalities, arguably the most well-known and popular celebrity in South Korea. He is also a member of SSAK3.
